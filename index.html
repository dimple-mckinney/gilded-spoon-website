Our Story

The Gilded Spoon is the sweet creation of Flour Shoppe Studios. It began with a simple idea: that a cake can be more than just a dessert—it can be a centerpiece, a conversation starter, and a work of art. At the studio, we blend time-honored baking techniques with a love for storytelling and design.

From our kitchen to yours, we pour our hearts into every creation, hoping to add a little bit of magic and sweetness to your life's special moments.
